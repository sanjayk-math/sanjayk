About Me

I am currently pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy in Mathematics at The Gandhigram Rural Institute-Deemed to be University, Gandhigram, Dindigul, specializing in the stabilization analysis of nonlinear dynamical systems through advanced quantum-inspired optimization techniques. With a solid foundation in applied mathematics, my research focuses on nonlinear systems, stability analysis, and innovative quantum computing applications to tackle complex challenges in system dynamics. My work has been published in high-impact journals, showcasing studies on quantum genetic algorithms for system control and resilient filter design against cyber threats. I am passionate about bridging quantum computing and classical mathematical approaches to deliver solutions for real-world problems, advancing both theoretical understanding and practical applications. Proficient in Python, Scilab, and LaTex, I bring expertise in analytical problem-solving and computational modelling. With a collaborative mindset and a commitment to continuous learning, I am eager to push the boundaries of applied mathematics and make meaningful contributions to the field.
